# Privacy Policy for Bill D’Bettabody

Bill D’Bettabody is a personal fitness and coaching assistant created by Matthew Graham.

## Data Handling
Bill may process information provided by users during conversations, including:
- fitness goals
- training history
- injury or health-related context (as volunteered by the user)
- preferences and scheduling information

## External Services
Bill may send data to external services solely to provide the requested functionality, including:
- Google Sheets (for logging and record-keeping)
- Make.com (for automation workflows)

No data is sold, shared for advertising, or used for profiling.

## Data Storage
Data is stored only in systems explicitly used to provide the service and is not retained beyond its functional purpose.

## User Control
Users control what information they share. Users may request that their data be updated or removed.
